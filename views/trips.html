Voyages

Despite his passion for the peripheries of the North Sea, Claaszen have accomplished several windsurfing crossings of Baltic sea.

Those included both short distance sprints as well as several days marathons. Typically, a "short" was an east-west passage between Sweden and Finland, that he usually managed to accomplish within 24 hours. On the other hand, the great North-South ride started in the Gulf of Bothnia and finished in the Vistula Bay. These took ten days at minimum. Occasionally, he would organise a Grand Slalom between the Aland Islands, including camping on uninhabited islands.
